###Customization
####Default wallpaper
The ```framework-res.apk``` contain the ```default_wallpaper.jpg```
```text
    /system/framework/framework-res.apk
    /res/drawable-nodpi/default_wallpaper.jpg
```
Check the size to select.

Click to download.
